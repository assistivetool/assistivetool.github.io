AssistiveTool

A new day in age for the visually impaired.



The objective.

  • Create easy to use assistive technology
  • Completely Free & Open Source software components
  • Flexibly usable in all environments you can imagine
  • Compatibility to a lot of hardware and Operating Systems
  • Best-in-class documentation and extensibility

The motivation.

  • Current assistive technology costs a lot of money
  • Owners are usually unable to tinker with their proprietary technology
  • Getting it right for everybody while keeping everything simple
  • Empowering people to live an independent lifestyle
